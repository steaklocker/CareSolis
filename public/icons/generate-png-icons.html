🧪 Testing on iPhone:

  1. Open Safari on iPhone (NOT Chrome)
  2. Navigate to your app URL
  3. Tap the Share button (square with arrow)
  4. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  5. Tap "Add" in the top right
  6. Find the CareSolis icon on your home screen
  7. Tap it → Should launch FULL SCREEN (no Safari UI)

If you still see Safari UI:

  • Delete the app from home screen
  • Clear Safari cache (Settings → Safari → Clear History and Website Data)
  • Try adding to home screen again
